Pumpkin spice cupcakes recipe

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1 cup pure pumpkin
2 tbsp molasses
1 tsp pure vanilla extract
1 stick of unsalted butter, at room temperature
3/4 cup granulated sugar
2 large eggs

Frosting ingredients:
4 oz cream cheese, at room temperature
2 tbsp butter, at room temperature
2 cups confectioners’ sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract

Method:

  • Heat oven to 350 °F and line a 12-cup muffin pan with paper liners.
  • Whisk flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in one bowl and pumpkin, molasses, and vanilla in another.
  • Beat butter and sugar with an electric mixer. Reduce speed and beat in eggs. Then add flour and pumpkin mixture.
  • Divide batter among muffin cups and bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
  • For frosting, beat cream cheese and butter using an electric mixer. Gradually beat in sugar and vanilla extract. Frost cupcakes with frosting and add pumpkin cupcake toppers.
