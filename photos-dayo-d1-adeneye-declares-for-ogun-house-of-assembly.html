Dayo D1 Adeneye Declares For Ogun House Of Assembly

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  223 words  |  Aldo Pusey

monjola sotubo

November 11, 2014 10:25 PM

The media personality is seeking to represent the Odogbolu Constituency at the Ogun State House of Assembly.

Kennis Music boss, Dayo “D1” Adeneye today, November 11, officially declared his intention to contest in the 2015 elections.

The media personality is seeking to represent the Odogbolu Constituency at the Ogun State House of Assembly.

“D1” officially announced his aspirations in Odogbolu and will be contesting on the platform of the All Progressives Congress (APC).
