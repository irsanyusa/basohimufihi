Weve Found 13 Celebrity Granddads Who Could Easily Steal Your Girlfriends Heart

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  768 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Men can be the strictest fathers, but then completely change their tune when they become granddads. And celebrities are no exception — they turn from these brutal thieves of female hearts into cute granddads, who like to pamper their grandkids and let them get away with anything.

We at Bright Side have dug through the biographies of these famous heart-stealers and found out that many of them have already become grandparents. At the end of the article, you’ll learn about Hollywood’s most famous great-grandmother.

1. Snoop Dogg and his huge family

Snoop Dogg’s name is closely associated with beautiful women, expensive cars, and massive gold and diamond jewelry. However, in real life, he has a lot of kids and is the caring granddad to 2 grandkids. He regularly shares their photos on social media, leaving his fans in awe.

2. Till Lindemann and his grandson Fritz

German rock star, Till Lindemann, became a single dad at the age of 22. He raised his older daughter, Nele, alone for quite a long time until he got married for a second time. This front man had a second daughter during his second marriage named, Marie Louise. In 2007, the musician became a granddad. His grandson Fritz Fidel is a big fan of his band, Rammstein.

3. Nicolas Cage and his older grandson Lucian

Actor and producer Nicolas Cage has 2 grandkids. In 2014, his first grandson, Lucian, was born, and in 2016, his second grandchild, Sorin, was born. Despite his hectic schedule, Cage tries to maintain constant communication with his grandkids and helps them out financially.

4. Kid Rock and his granddaughter Skye

Kid Rock became a grandfather at the age of 43. After Skye’s birth, the musician settled down and stopped getting into trouble. Rock adores his granddaughter. He takes her to Disneyland, travels with her, gives her presents, and shares their photos together on social media.

5. Jim Carrey and his grandson Jackson

The famous Hollywood comedian became a granddad at the age of 48. Carrey admits that his grandson became the biggest happiness in his life. “I am happy that I have a grandson. Once I was playing with him and realized that I will die without him!” the actor wrote on his Twitter.

6. Eddie Murphy, the young grandfather

Eddie Murphy has a huge family. He has 4 sons and 6 daughters. Recently, he also became a grandfather. In July 2019 the actor’s daughter-in-law gave birth to a girl named Evie Isla. The baby has been surrounded by love and care since her birth, while her photos have flooded her young parents’ Instagram.

7. Tom Hanks, a grandpa to 3

Tom Hanks has 3 grandkids: 2 charming granddaughters from his older son Colin and a 3-year-old granddaughter from his younger son Chet. His children claim that Hanks is a loving and caring granddad, while the girls don’t even suspect that he is a big Hollywood celebrity.

8. Sting’s grandson

Sting is another father with a big family in the world of show business. He has 6 kids and 4 grandkids. The musician believes he was a bad father, but became a good grandfather. “I became a more responsible granddad than a father. I love all my kids, but you know, I was a rock star. Now I am a simple granddad,” the musician admitted in one of his interviews.

9. Harrison Ford, the perfect grandfather

Harrison has 3 grandsons and one granddaughter: Eliel, Guiliana, Ethan Ford, and Waylon. The actor could be called Hollywood’s most exemplary grandfather. He always finds time for his family in his hectic work schedule. Together they attend charity dinners, social events, and have picnics and family gatherings.

10. Charlie Sheen and his granddaughter Luna

Charlie Sheen became a grandfather at the age of 47. In 2013, he welcomed his granddaughter Luna to this world. The actor admits that he has changed a lot after becoming granddad, and his life has split into before and after. Sheen adores his little Luna and tries to spend as much time as possible with her.

11. Sean Bean’s grandson

The British knockout, Sean Bean, has been married 5 times and he has 3 daughters. In 2015, he became a grandfather — his middle daughter, Molly, became a mother. 2 years later, his oldest daughter, Lorna, also became a mother and gave birth to Bean’s second grandkid.

12. Pierce Brosnan and his granddaughter Marley May

Pierce Brosnan has 5 kids and 3 grandkids, He first became a grandfather at the age of 44. However, it’s Marley May who can be called the actor’s favorite. She spends a lot of time with her famous granddad, while all the actor’s fans continue to admire her photos on Brosnan’s Instagram.

13. Dave Bautista and his grandsons

Not only is Dave Bautista a famous actor and athlete, but he is also a young grandfather. When he was 40, he welcomed his 2 grandsons Jacob and Aiden. The boys adore their celebrity granddad and he is a real superhero for them.

Bonus: Whoopi Goldberg, the great-grandmother

Whoopi Goldberg became a grandmother at the age of 34, when her 16-year-old daughter Alexandrea gave birth to a girl named Amarah. A quarter-century later, the actress welcomed her great-granddaughter. Goldberg shared this joyful piece of news with her followers on her Instagram page, “Everyone, meet the new addition to my family, Charli Rose, and yea I’m a tad awkward, it took me a minute, been a LONG time!”

Every grandfather is cool in his own way, no matter whether he is a celebrity or not. What were your granddads like?
