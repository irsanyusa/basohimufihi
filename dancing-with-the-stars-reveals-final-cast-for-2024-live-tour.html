The Dancing With The Stars Live Tour 2024 cast is officially complete. The dancing competition series’ official account for tour revealed the final lineup of pros and celebrities joining the tour next year.

Final Cast for Dancing With The Stars 2024 Tour Revealed

In a recent post on Instagram, DWTS announced their additional guest stars and dancing pros for the tour. New guests include season 32 alums Charity Lawson, Harry Jowsey, and Xochitl Gomez. Meanwhile, in select dates, pros Artem Chigvintsev, Jenna Johnson, and Valentin Chmerkovskiy will also grace the tour.

“We’re thrilled to announce that @charitylawson, @harryjowsey, @_xochitl.gomez & @juleshough are joining #DWTSTour this winter! PLUS, @theartemc, @jennajohnson & @valentin join our incredible cast of pros for select dates,” the caption read.

DWTS co-host Julianne Hough will also serve as special guest. She will perform from February 15 to February 18, March 1 to March 3, and March 26.

Meanwhile, Season 32 Mirrorball champion Gomez will perform from January 11 to February 4. Lawson, will then perform from February 6 to February 18. Jowsey, on the other hand, will perform from February to March 26.

Johnson and Chmerkovskiy will perform from January 11 to February 4. Lastly, Chigvintsev will grace the tour from February 6 to February 18.

“See the ballroom brought to life in this brand-new production featuring your favorite Dancing with the Stars pros, PLUS special guest stars! With dazzling routines in every style, jaw-dropping talent, and non-stop entertainment, it’s sure to be an unforgettable night full of all the magic of the TV show and more!” the official description of tour reads.

Xochitl Gomez Says Her Mirrorball Triumph is “Unbelievable”

Alongside Chmerkovskiy, Gomez snatched the first ever Len Goodman Mirrorball Trophy. Following Tuesday’s finale, Gomez reflected on her season 32 victory.

“It’s unbelievable,” she said. “I didn’t see it coming. I went into this whole competition thing thinking, you know, if I make it to week five, that’d be awesome, and so to be here and to have this trophy is just crazy. I’m really proud of us.”

For his part, Chmerkovskiy “felt really good.” According to the DWTS pro, the experience also felt a bit weird.

“It’s a little weird, you know, because you put so much work into something and then it’s like here’s a trophy and then it’s over,” he said. “The feeling is, obviously pride and joy for ourselves, but also a little bit of sadness that it’s over.”

According to the duo, Chmerkovskiy’s wife and fellow pro Johnson played a significant role in helping them achieve their win on the show.

“She’s a part of every creative, every step, every piece of choreography that we performed this season, she was a part of it,” Chmerkovskiy said.

Furthermore, Gomez stated that Johnson has been “encouraging” during her stint. She went on to share that Chmerkovkiy’s wife helped her when she felt “scared and nervous” with the freestyle part of her performance.
