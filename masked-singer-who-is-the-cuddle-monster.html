The Masked Singer is back and better than ever with Trolls Night! The Candelabra, Anteater, and Donut will be taking the stage again, joined by the “BIGGEST costume ever,” the Cuddle Monster. And with that new addition to the Monster family, we’re in for a night of rainbow and sparkles!

But who is the celebrity under the Cuddle Monster mask? Let’s go through all the clues and performances for the Cuddle Monster and find out.

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 10

The Cuddle Monster started his clues by saying that he was a wild child, while lightning strikes the ground in front of him. He claimed that he “grew up in a beautiful but sometimes violent place” but luckily he got out. We see eyes in the sky. He got out “because of [his] passion,” a generational talent. The clues then showed a bubbling bride, shoes, and boxing gloves in the air while Cuddle Monster mentioned he had sponsorship deals, sneakers, and even a record or two.

He was self-destructive during the peak of his career, he explained while a gold bull trophy appeared on the screen. The Cuddle Monster couldn’t figure out why he was always happy and defensive, so he finally got help. All he needed was “a change of perspective and persona.” The Trolls then gave a clue of a glowing globe with “CHAMP” written on it, as some people walked by with a stool and a shield.

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Cuddle Monster

Robin predicted that Cuddle Monster could be Dennis Rodman. Ken agreed it could be a basketball star, throwing out Draymon Green as his guess. Nicole thought it could be Shaq, based on his size and past music career! The most popular guesses online included Shaquille O’Neal, Michael Strahan, Mike Tyson, James Corden, or (in one of the most hilarious predictions yet) Kevin Hart. Wouldn’t it be amazing to see him in this GIANT costume? I love that guess.

Who is the Cuddle Monster on The Masked Singer?

With that singing voice, unfortunately Cuddle Monster didn’t last long on The Masked Singer stage. On Trolls Night, he was revealed to be…

Metta World Peace!

The Lakers and basketball legend makes sense, in a costume that large! And there are plenty more wild reveals coming this season I’m sure. So don’t miss The Masked Singer, Wednesdays at 8/7c on FOX!
