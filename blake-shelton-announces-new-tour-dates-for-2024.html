Former The Voice coach Blake Shelton is going on the road again next year. The country star just announced new dates for his Back to the Honky Tonk Tour, starting in February. Tickets go on sale next week.

Blake Shelton Announces 2024 Tour Dates

Shelton first kicked off his Back to the Honky Tonk Tour earlier this year, with special guests Carly Pearce and Jackson Dean. He recently announced that he’s added new dates to the tour for 2024, beginning February 22 in Hershey, Pennsylvania.

The star will perform in various cities around the United States and Canada, including Detroit, Austin, Edmonton, Portland, and Palm Springs. This time around, he’ll be joined by special guests Dustin Lynch and Emily Ann Roberts.

Tickets will be available to the public starting next Friday, October 27 (except Glendale, which goes on sale November 3). Fan presale starts Tuesday, October 24. You can register for access on Shelton’s website. Presale for Glendale opens October 31.

Shelton teased the tour on Instagram earlier this month. He even hinted at the new opening acts by posting Lynch and Roberts’ initials.

Shelton Left The Voice Earlier This Year

Shelton has more time to tour these days since he left his coaching position on The Voice earlier this year. The country star appeared on the show for the first 23 seasons (and won nine of them) before deciding it was time to walk away.

The show is currently airing Season 24, as Shelton’s wife Gwen Stefani is sitting on the coaching panel for her first season without him. The pair met on the show in Season 7, back in 2014. They began dating the next year and tied the knot in 2021.
