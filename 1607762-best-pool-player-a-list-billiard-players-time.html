  • Full name: Shane Van Boening
  • Career: American professional pool player
  • Born: 14 July 1983

10. Kelly Fisher

  • Full name: Kelly Teresa Fisher MBE
  • Career: English professional pool, snooker, and billiards player
  • Born: 25 August 1978
  • Age: 45 years (as of April 2024)
  • Birthplace: South Elmsall, United Kingdom

Kelly Fisher is among the top 10 greatest billiard players of all time. She started as a snooker player, winning three Ladies World Snooker Championships between 1998 and 2000. 9. Johnny Archer

  • Full name: Johnny Archer
  • Career: American professional pool player
  • Born: 12 November 1968
  • Age: 55 years (as of April 2024)
  • Birthplace: Waycross, Georgia, United States

Johnny is nicknamed "the Scorpion" because one of his sponsors is Scorpion Cues and his zodiac sign, Scorpio. Having achieved titles such as the WPA World Nine-ball Championship and Sudden Death Seven-ball and more than 60 professional tournament victories, Johnny Archer joined the Billiard Congress of America Hall of Fame on 8 June 2009.

7. Mike Sigel

  • Full name: Michael Sigel
  • Career: American professional pool player
  • Born: 1 July 1952
  • Age: 71 years (as of April 2024)
  • Birthplace: Rochester, New York, United States

The American shooter has won over 102 major tournaments in his active career. The New Yorker dominated the sport in the 70s and 80s and earned the nickname "Captain Hook" for his ambidextrous ability to use safety plays to hook opponents. Michael Sigel became the youngest player to earn his place in the Billiard Congress of America Hall of Fame at age 35 in 1989.

  • Full name: Rudolf Walter Wanderone

3. Willie Mosconi

  • Full name: William Joseph Mosconi
  • Career: American professional pool player
  • Born: 27 June 1913
  • Birthplace: United States
  • Died: 17 September 1993

The charismatic American billiard shooter was considered one of the most prolific players of his active time between 1919 and 1937. He won the World Pocket Billiards Championship twenty times. Ralph Greenleaf was one of the first three inaugural inductees into the BCA Hall of Fame in 1966.

Efren Manalang Reyes, aka Bata, is considered the greatest pool player of all time by fans and mates.
