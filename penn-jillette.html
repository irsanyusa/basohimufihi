Penn Jillette (Magician and Author)

 Posted on May 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  179 words  |  Martina Birk

Full Name: Penn Fraser Jillette
Profession: Magician and Author

Biography: Jillette is a magician best known as one half of Penn & Teller, who as a pair have featured across numerous television and stage shows: 'Penn & Teller: Fool Us', 'Penn & Teller: Bullshit!', etc. They are currently headlining together for The Rio in Las Vegas.

Along with his performance work, Jillette has also published several books, including the New York Times Bestseller: 'God, No!: Signs You May Already Be an Atheist and Other Magical Tales.' He is known in this regard for his staunch atheism and scientific skepticism.

Born: March 5, 1955
