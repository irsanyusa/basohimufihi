Ashley Graham Revealed Why She Needles Her Face and Butt Every Day

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  418 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ashley Graham has an interesting way of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Instead of worrying about getting a full 8 hours of sleep, working out for an hour every day, or trying to perfectly balance work and life, she focuses on something different. For Ashley, what matters most are happiness and laughter. She also prefers using natural beauty methods instead of chemical-based ones. Combining joy and natural treatments has a wonderful effect, making her feel and look great.

Embracing her imperfections

Ashley Graham, the renowned supermodel and advocate for body positivity, recently went on TikTok to share an exciting update with her 1.8 million followers. The 35-year-old beauty, known for her empowering messages and stunning looks, revealed her latest wellness obsession: acupuncture.

It all began when Ashley fearlessly embraced her imperfections, especially after becoming a mother. Determined to address what she could, she embarked on an exhilarating one-week wellness journey guided by her close friend Sandra, a practitioner of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

Documenting the experience

She documented her wellness week in 4 parts, which she shared with her fans on her TikTok account. Although the needle treatment was not unfamiliar to her, she joyfully spoke about it, making it seem like a new experience.

In her first video of the week, Ashley carried a black massage table and sheets, capturing the attention of eager viewers who wanted to follow her acupuncture journey. She explained that her sessions would incorporate various traditional Chinese practices, including gua sha, cupping, moxa, and, of course, the insertion of acupuncture needles.

She addressed any skeptics or those who might be uncomfortable with these methods, sharing that she preferred natural alternatives over chemical-based options, though she jokingly expressed openness to a little Botox.

With a thoughtful trigger warning in her caption, Ashley respected her audience’s sensitivity to the sight of needles. She emphasized the profound impact these practices have had on her well-being and enthusiastically endorsed their potential to work wonders for individuals of all body types.

Unveiling the needles’ secrets

Ashley Graham’s fascination with natural wellness and beauty has been a consistent theme throughout her life. Her commitment to non-invasive procedures aligns with her philosophy of embracing her body and making choices that correspond with her values.

As a firm believer in the power of holistic healing, Ashley has found comfort in acupuncture and its ability to harness the body’s natural resources for rejuvenation.

In her ongoing exploration of wellness, Ashley continues to inspire her followers to consider alternative approaches to self-care. Through her TikTok updates, she sparks curiosity and encourages individuals to venture outside their comfort zones, discovering what works best for their own bodies.

With her authenticity and unwavering commitment to empowering others, Ashley Graham has become influential in promoting self-love and embracing unique beauty.

Ashley has shown her confidence many times before this, and a great example would be her clothing-free photoshoot just 4 months after she gave birth.
