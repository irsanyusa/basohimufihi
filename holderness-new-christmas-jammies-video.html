Holderness Family Christmas Video | TIME

December 16, 2015 3:59 PM EST

The family that has become famous on the Internet for dancing in pajamas has released a new video for the holidays. And, of course, they are dancing in pajamas.

In an approximately three-minute parody of Kendrick Lamar’s “i” called “Elfed,” former TV journalists Penn Holderness and his wife Kim, along with their eight-year-old daughter Lola and 5-year-old son Penn Charles, dress up like elves and dance for people a block away from Radio City Music Hall.

The Raleigh, North Carolina, family first went viral two years ago with “Christmas Jammies,” a music video in which former news anchor Penn Holderness rapped about getting a vasectomy in red-and-green-striped pajamas. The antics were so goofy that they inspired a Saturday Night Live sketch.
