Best friends Sadie Marquardt and Kaya Munn had an unforgettable ‘AGT’ audition. They recieved a warm welcome from the judges and the audience even before they performed their belly dancing routine. The Belly Dancing Duo had everyone mesmerized with their stunning looks, especially Howie Mandel. Check out their Season 5 audition below.

Belly Dancing Duo Mesmerizes ‘AGT’ Judges Before They Even Perform

Immediately when Sadie and Kaya stepped out onto the stage, Howie was grinning from ear to ear. When judge Piers Morgan began to welcome them to ‘AGT,’ Howie said “Wait Piers just give us a minute.” He stared at them and let out a laugh.

“It’s a yes from me” Piers joked even before the act began. “You’re going to Vegas” Howie added. The audience was eager to see The Belly Dancing Duo perform with their sexy outfits on. They danced to a very fast-paced drum solo. Howie could not wipe the smile off of his face throughout the routine.

When it came time for the judges to give critiques, Howie was so flustered he couldn’t even find the right words to say. Finally, he said that Sadie and Kaya are “overflowing with talent” and the roaring audience seemed to agree. Sharon Osbourne complimented their “fantastic” belly dancing. Howie joked that he didn’t even notice the belly dancing. “He was just looking at the t and t,” Sharon said about Howie.

“The talent and the timing” Howie added before the audience burst into laughter again. The judges gave The Belly Dancing Duo three “yes’s” with Piers saying he could’ve watched their act for months. They advanced to the Quarterfinals where they landed in the Judge’s Choice after another sexy dance. They didn’t get enough judge’s votes to advance on to the Semifinals.

Where Are They Now?

After appearing on ‘AGT,’ Sadie and Kaya have continued on with their very successful belly dancing careers for over a decade. This includes teaching classes, going on tour and posting videos. Sadie has 112,000 Instagram followers and her own business called Raqs Online where she teaches online classes. Kaya has her own photography business and an extensive belly dancing resume as well.

Sadie also launched a line of dancewear. She is one of the most-watched belly dancers on Youtube, racking up millions of views with her intricate videos. Sadie has performed her belly dancing act and held workshops in over 40 countries.
