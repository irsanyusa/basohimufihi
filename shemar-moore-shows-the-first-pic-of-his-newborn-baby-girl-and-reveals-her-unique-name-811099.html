Shemar Moore Shows the First Pic of His Newborn Baby Girl and Reveals Her Unique Name

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  298 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Shemar Moore first made his relationship with Jesiree Dizon public when he announced in early January that they were expecting their first child together. And it’s time for a celebration! Shemar Moore is officially a dad, and he revealed the news in the sweetest way. Let’s get to know the couple and their newborn baby.

Dreams do come true for Moore.

Dan Steinberg/Invision/AP/East News

Shemar Moore revealed that he is having his first kid during an interview on The Jennifer Hudson Show, but he didn’t publicly reveal his partner Jesiree Dizon to the world until he posted a video of the gender reveal party the couple hosted on January 9th.

Moore’s representative revealed that the couple had given birth to a child. “Shemar Moore and his partner, Jesiree Dizon, are happy to announce the birth of their baby girl. The family is very happy and healthy,” the spokesman stated.

They seem like they want to enlarge their family.

This is her first child with Moore, but Dizon already has 2 children from prior relationships. During the gender reveal party, Dizon can be heard saying “2 more” when Moore asks her if she wants more children. This suggests that she is open to having more children with Moore in the future. However, it is ultimately up to the couple to decide if they want to expand their family further.

Moore is undoubtedly so happy about being a daddy.

Moore said he’s “so excited” to become a parent during his visit on Hudson’s talk show. He stated: “I was worried for a while that maybe that ship had sailed kind of thing, and, you know, God had my back and things lined up...”

He took to Instagram to announce the baby’s name.

Moore shared the first photo of his girl, looking at her with joy and happiness. Her name is Frankie, Frankie Moore. The baby was born on January 24th, weighing 7.1 pounds, and was 20 inches long. He described the picture: “BABY GIRL in da building!!! Already THE LOVE OF MY LIFE!! I’m a Girl Daddy! DREAMS COME TRUE!”
